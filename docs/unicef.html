Unicef

48 hour project

In this three day project Unicef gave Hyper Island students a brief to increase participants of Operation Dagsverke, targeting the student council. We wanted to make Operation Dagsverke more attractive and fun and decided therefore to make it as an exclusive invitation for the students only and more of a fun game feel.
