Society Hack

48 hour project

Transdev and Holland & Philipsson approach Hyper Island students to help them come up with a better sustainable solution for public transportation. After an intense three days work we ended up with Qnect as a different way of travelling easier, causing less hassle or traffic jams during rush hour. In this project I designed the logo as well as being part of the teams ideation and production.
